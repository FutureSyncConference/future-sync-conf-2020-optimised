about

16th & 17th April 2020, 10:00am - 4pm UK time
Broadcast online from South West UK

Future Sync is a tech conference keeping you in-sync with the latest digital trends.
Join us online on the 16th & 17th April 2020 for two days of talks alongside plenty of distributed networking opportunities throughout the event.

Broadcast from Plymouth, UK, the day will encourage the exchange of ideas and knowledge whilst helping to forge new partnerships. This is a great opportunity to join this popular technology conference from wherever you are around the world.

theme 1software development

Explore the latest trends in software development including immersive tech, web, native and hybrid apps and games.
